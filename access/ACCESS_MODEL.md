# Access Model Policy

## Definitions
- **Free** = permanently public, linkable, and safe to share
- **Paid** = distributed via storefront(s) or private release channels
- **Premium** = delivered through cohorts, certification, and consulting engagements

## What is always Free
1) Canonical Index
2) Sample Chapters
3) Glossary / Controlled Vocabulary

## What is Paid
1) Full volumes
2) Study guides
3) Workbook
4) Companion datasets (machine-readable)

## What is Premium
1) Cohort course
2) Certification
3) Consulting

## Canonical reference
This project maintains a canonical metadata source (titles, series, volume numbering, identifiers, official links).
See `links/CANONICAL_INDEX.md`.
